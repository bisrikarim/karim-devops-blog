## Who I am

DevSecOps & Automation Engineer with 8+ years of experience designing, securing, and automating infrastructure at scale. Currently based in Casablanca, Morocco.

Specialized in Kubernetes, Terraform, Ansible, and GitLab CI/CD. My focus has been on building reliable, secure, and observable platforms — from on-premise bare metal to hybrid cloud environments.

## What I work on

At [Sofrecom](https://www.sofrecom.com/), I design and automate infrastructure using a GitOps-first approach. This means declarative configuration, continuous reconciliation, and zero-manual-drift environments.

My current interests sit at the intersection of classic DevSecOps and AI-applied automation — specifically:

- **LLM integration in CI/CD pipelines** — automated code review, documentation generation, log analysis
- **Sovereign AI tooling** — on-premise LLM workflows using Ollama, keeping sensitive code and infra data off public APIs
- **Platform Engineering** — building internal developer platforms that reduce cognitive load and accelerate delivery

## Philosophy

> Automate what's repetitive. Document what's complex. Secure everything.

I believe infrastructure should be boring in production — predictable, observable, and easy to reason about. That requires investing heavily in automation, testing, and GitOps practices up front.
